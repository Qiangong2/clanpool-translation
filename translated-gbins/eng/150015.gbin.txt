"Underwater Movement" is a cyber app that allows you
to move underwater. It will automatically start when you
enter a well, and will take you to the lower floor.
―――――――――――――――――――――――――――――――――――――――― 10001
When underwater, power is used whenever you walk
a single square. If you run out of power, you will start
to suffocate and take damage.
